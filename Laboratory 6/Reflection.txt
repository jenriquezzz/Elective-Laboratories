Our group meeting was planned during our face-to-face classes where we discussed possible schedules and how we would conduct the meeting. We also communicated through online instant messaging to coordinate with each other, and to confirm everyone’s free time. Since all of us enjoy playing games, we decided to hold the google meeting as an online gaming event so that everyone could participate.

However, our first attempt at the meeting was unsuccessful because some members had unexpected emergency events to attend. This caused delays and forced us to reschedule the discussion. One of the main challenges we experienced was coordinating everyone’s schedules.

From this experience, we learned that online collaboration can sometimes be more difficult than face-to-face collaboration because it may lead to lower commitment. We realized that clear communication and proper planning are important to make online collaboration work better.
