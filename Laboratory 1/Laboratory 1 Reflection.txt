The design principle I found the most useful when designing the Canva was keeping the slide organized so the information was easy to follow. Canva made it simple to arrange text and images especially with its preset layouts and alignment tools. What took more time was deciding on fonts and colors that looked clean without drawing too much attention. Working on the slides made me more aware about how layout affects understanding. The way information is spaced and grouped can make a big difference in how clearly it is communicated.
